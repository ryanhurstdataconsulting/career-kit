# Optional: tell your story on Poe first

**You don't need this page.** Everything in your kit works beautifully without
Poe — the interview, the job matching, the tailored resumes, all of it runs
right inside the Claude app. Skip this page unless you're curious.

It might appeal to you if you already enjoy chatting on poe.com, or if you'd
like to sketch out your story on your phone — on the couch, on a walk — before
you sit down for the real `/intake` interview.

## The idea

Open **[poe.com/Claude-Fable-5](https://poe.com/Claude-Fable-5)** and have a
relaxed, free-form conversation that covers the same ground the `/intake`
interview will. There's no form to fill out — just talk it through. Try to
touch each of these, roughly in this order:

1. **Warm-up** — what kind of work you're looking for, in your own words.
2. **Education** — schools, degrees, dates, anything you're proud of.
3. **Career history** — for each role: company, title, dates, what you actually
   did, and wins with numbers where you have them.
4. **Target roles** — where you want to go next, and at what level.
5. **Example jobs** — three to five links to postings you'd genuinely love.
6. **Compensation** — your target range, your hard minimum, and the currency.
7. **Location and work authorization** — city, timezone, remote or hybrid or
   onsite, and your visa or work-authorization status.
8. **Culture** — what you're drawn to and what you're avoiding.
9. **Practical application answers** — sponsorship, notice period, willingness
   to relocate, and the like.

### A starter prompt you can paste

> Hi! I'm getting ready for a job search, and I'd love your help thinking it
> through out loud. I'll tell you about my background — my education, the jobs
> I've had, and the kind of role I want next — and I'd like you to ask me
> friendly follow-up questions along the way, one at a time. Please also nudge
> me to share a target salary range, where I want to work (and whether I need
> visa sponsorship), the culture I'm after, and a few links to jobs I'd love.
> When we're done, could you gather everything into one tidy summary I can save?
> Let's go one topic at a time — start by asking what kind of work I'm looking
> for.

## Bringing it home

When you're happy with the conversation, copy it (or use Poe's export) into a
plain text file or a PDF, and drop that file into the **`intake/`** folder in
your kit. Then run **`/intake`** in the Claude app. The interview reads whatever
you dropped in and pre-fills from it — confirming details with you instead of
asking you to retype everything.

Nothing you do on Poe has to be complete or polished. A few rough notes are
plenty; `/intake` fills in the rest.

## For the technically curious

Poe also offers an OpenAI-compatible API at `https://api.poe.com/v1` (you can
generate a key at [poe.com/api_keys](https://poe.com/api_keys), using the model
`Claude-Fable-5`), and calls spend your Poe subscriber points. Your kit
deliberately does **not** wire this in. The Claude app is the engine, and one
assistant that holds all of your context beats two half-informed ones.

## A quick privacy note

Anything you share on poe.com is governed by Poe's terms and privacy policy —
not by your kit's local-only, nothing-leaves-this-computer guarantee. Share
there the way you'd share with any online service, and keep the truly sensitive
details for the real `/intake` interview.
