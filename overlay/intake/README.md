# Drop zone for /intake

Put your current resume (PDF or Word) and, if you like, your LinkedIn profile
export (PDF) in this folder before running `/intake`. Claude reads them first
so it can confirm details with you instead of asking you to retype your whole
history.

To export your LinkedIn profile: open your profile → **More** → **Save to PDF**.

Nothing in this folder is ever committed to git or sent anywhere — it stays on
this computer.
